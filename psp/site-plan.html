Overview

Purpose

We believe that, at its best, a barbershop is a reliable and trustworthy neighborhood institution. Our website seeks to connect people with our company and show how important it is for us to provide everyone with a comfortable space where they can enjoy the grooming services offered by our barber shop and that our main goal is to establish solid and lasting relationships with each of our clients.

Audience

Typically, good looks can boost self-confidence and self-esteem. If you are looking for a change of style, a comfortable and familiar place where you can enjoy good care or even friends, this is the right place.

Home page

WE RESPECT THE HERITAGE OF CLASSIC BARBERING. Dare to be different and get ready to shine! We take tremendous pride in our work and perpetuating the fine art of classic barbering. Our strength and differentiation is manifest in the details: how intently we listen to what you want, how well the hair is tapered, how straight the neckline is, how even the sideburns are. You can't find a finer men's haircut in Trelew. We would love the opportunity to be your preferred barber and help you "Live like a King".

About Us

We believe that going to the barber shop should be more than an errand. It should be an experience. It's important to us that you get a great haircut, receive remarkable service and build lasting relationships in a comfortable space. We offer more than a typical barbershop. Boardroom combines a relaxing, sophisticated environment and a friendly, professional team to deliver the ultimate grooming experience. We are committed to being an inclusive and welcoming environment for every client and member of our team who enter our space. We celebrate the diversity within our community and aim to be an example of unity in The King's Barber Shop. Our store is one to impress everyone who comes to it for the first time. We guarantee exceptional client service to every client, on every visit. With a welcoming atmosphere, we thrive to become the most trusted hair salon destination in Trelew.
